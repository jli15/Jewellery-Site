Who are we?

Magia, meaning magic in Italian, is exactly what are have envisioned in our branding image. Our dream is to become tomorrow's trendsetters. Our vision is to produce high quality jewelry that the buyer will wear with passion and desire, thus leading to our goal of manufacturing contemporary and bold concepts which will be reborn as the classics of tomorrow.

Since 2002...
Founded in 2002, Magia Limited is an exporter, importer and wholesaler of fine jewelry and jewelry materials. The products we sell included precious stones and diamond jewelry, emerald jewelry, ruby jewelry, sapphire jewelry and etc.
